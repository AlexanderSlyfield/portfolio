About

I am a software developer with a strong interest in building well-structured and maintainable applications. I recently completed an intensive Full Stack Developer course with IT Career Switch and Codecademy, where I developed a strong foundation in modern web technologies and software development practices. Throughout the course and in my own time, I’ve built a range of projects that strengthened my problem-solving skills and deepened my understanding of how real applications are designed and developed. I particularly enjoy being involved in projects which span across the full development lifecycle, as it interests me to learn how applications evolve from ideas into fully functioning applications. I'm driven by the the satisfaction of creating software that is both functional and well designed and continue to expand my skills as I work towards a professional career in software development.

Software Developer

This mindset of reflection and iteration is something I bring to my development work as I continue building and refining my skills.
